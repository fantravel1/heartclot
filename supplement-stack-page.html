The Evidence-Based Supplement Stack

After 35 years and 30,000+ patients, here's what actually works – and the popular supplements that are killing you

⚠️ STOP Taking Calcium Supplements

Multiple studies show calcium supplements increase heart attack risk by 20-30%. The problem isn't calcium – it's taking isolated calcium without the cofactors that direct it properly.

Why Calcium Supplements Are Killing You

Women are told to take calcium for bones. Men take it "just in case." Both are accelerating arterial calcification.

Watch: The calcium supplement disaster [1:08:56-1:09:31]

The Calcium Paradox

  • Young women in Florida with osteopenia despite calcium supplements
  • Calcium added to milk, orange juice, everything
  • Still have weak bones – why?
  • Missing cofactors: D3 and K2
  • Calcium goes to arteries instead of bones

How Calcium Becomes Deadly

  1. Without D3: Poor absorption from gut
  2. Without K2: Calcium deposits in arteries
  3. Result: Arterial calcification + weak bones
  4. Irony: Taking calcium causes what you're trying to prevent
Research Shows: Large studies demonstrate 20-30% increased cardiovascular events with calcium supplements, especially doses above 1,000mg daily.

Vitamin D3: The Foundation

Vitamin D3 (Cholecalciferol)

Dose: 5,000 IU daily (125 mcg)

With food: Fat-soluble, take with meals

Why You're Deficient:

  • Indoor lifestyle
  • Sunscreen blocks production
  • Darker skin = less production
  • Age reduces synthesis
  • Obesity sequesters D3

Cardiovascular Benefits:

  • Improves calcium absorption from food
  • Reduces inflammation
  • Lowers blood pressure
  • Improves insulin sensitivity
  • Supports immune function

Testing:

  • Test 25-hydroxyvitamin D
  • Optimal: 50-80 ng/mL
  • Below 30 = deficient
  • Retest every 3-6 months initially

Vitamin K2: The Missing Link

Vitamin K2 (MK-7 form)

Dose: 200 mcg daily

Timing: With D3 and fat

The Arterial Protection Factor:

  • Activates osteocalcin (pulls calcium INTO bones)
  • Activates MGP (pulls calcium OUT of arteries)
  • Prevents arterial calcification
  • May reverse existing calcification

Why You Don't Get Enough:

  • Found in fermented foods (we don't eat)
  • Grass-fed animal products (not grain-fed)
  • Destroyed by antibiotics
  • Not in plant foods
Blood Thinner Alert: Warfarin (Coumadin) blocks K1 AND K2. This accelerates arterial calcification. Discuss alternatives with your doctor.

Omega-3: The Anti-Inflammatory

Omega-3 Fatty Acids (EPA/DHA)

Dose: 2-4g daily (combined EPA/DHA)

Form: Triglyceride form (not ethyl ester)

Critical for Vagus Nerve:

  • 50% of population deficient
  • DHA essential for nerve function
  • Improves heart rate variability
  • Reduces inflammation systemically

Cardiovascular Benefits:

  • Lowers triglycerides
  • Reduces blood pressure
  • Prevents arrhythmias
  • Improves endothelial function
  • Reduces clot formation

Quality Matters:

  • Third-party tested for mercury
  • IFOS certified preferred
  • Refrigerate after opening
  • Should not smell "fishy"

Magnesium: The Forgotten Mineral

Magnesium (Glycinate or Citrate)

Dose: 400-600mg at bedtime

Form: Glycinate for sleep, citrate if constipated

Why Everyone's Deficient:

  • Soil depletion = less in food
  • Stress depletes magnesium
  • Caffeine increases loss
  • Alcohol depletes stores
  • Medications (PPIs, diuretics)

Heart Benefits:

  • Regulates heart rhythm
  • Relaxes blood vessels
  • Improves insulin sensitivity
  • Reduces blood pressure
  • Prevents muscle cramps

Signs of Deficiency:

  • Muscle cramps/twitches
  • Insomnia
  • Anxiety
  • Constipation
  • Irregular heartbeat
