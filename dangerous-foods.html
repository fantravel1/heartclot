Why the "healthy" foods you're eating daily are destroying your arteries – and what to eat instead

The Vegetable Oil Inflammation Bomb

The biggest scam in nutrition history: "heart-healthy" vegetable oils that are actually industrial waste products.

The Omega-6 Disaster: Our ancestors had a 1:1 ratio of omega-6 to omega-3. Today's ratio? 20:1 or worse. This drives massive inflammation.

Oils That Destroy

  • Soybean oil: In everything, pure omega-6
  • Corn oil: Extracted with hexane (industrial solvent)
  • Canola oil: Highly processed, oxidizes easily
  • Sunflower oil: Unstable, creates aldehydes when heated
  • Safflower oil: 75% omega-6, pure inflammation
  • "Vegetable" oil: Mystery blend of the worst oils

What Happens When You Heat These Oils

  1. Molecular structure breaks down
  2. Creates toxic aldehydes and oxidized lipids
  3. These damage arterial walls on contact
  4. Triggers inflammatory cascade
  5. Small dense LDL forms
Safe Cooking Fats:
  • Butter/Ghee: Stable at high heat
  • Coconut oil: Saturated = stable
  • Extra virgin olive oil: For low-heat only
  • Avocado oil: High smoke point

White Rice: The Arsenic Problem Nobody Mentions

Rice absorbs arsenic from soil and water. Modern agriculture has made this worse.

Watch: The shocking truth about rice [1:07:15-1:08:20]

Arsenic Facts:
  • Rice has 10-20x more arsenic than other crops
  • Brown rice has MORE arsenic than white (in the bran)
  • Arsenic is a known poison that accumulates
  • Linked to cardiovascular disease and cancer

If You Must Eat Rice: The Safe Protocol

  1. Soak overnight: In 6:1 water to rice ratio
  2. Discard soak water: Removes 30% of arsenic
  3. Cook in fresh water: Use 10:1 ratio
  4. Drain cooking water: Like pasta
  5. Cool overnight: Creates resistant starch
  6. Reheat before eating: Lowers glycemic impact

Advanced Glycation End Products (AGEs)

When you cook food at high temperatures, you create molecules that don't exist in nature – and your body attacks them.

How AGEs Form

Protein + Fat + Sugar + High Heat = Toxic AGEs

❌ High AGE Foods

  • Grilled/charred meat
  • Fried anything
  • Roasted nuts
  • Toasted bread
  • BBQ with char marks
  • Crispy bacon
  • French fries
  • Roasted coffee beans

✅ Low AGE Cooking

  • Steaming
  • Boiling
  • Poaching
  • Slow cooking
  • Raw foods
  • Marinading in acid
  • Moist heat methods
  • Lower temperatures
The AGE Receptor Connection: Your body has RAGE receptors (Receptors for Advanced Glycation End products). When AGEs bind to RAGE, they trigger massive inflammation throughout your vascular system.

The Fruit Problem: Nature's Candy Gone Wrong

Modern fruit has been bred for sweetness. An apple today has 50% more sugar than 50 years ago.

Fructose Goes Straight to Your Liver: Unlike glucose, fructose can only be processed by your liver. Excess fructose = fatty liver = metabolic dysfunction.

Fruit Reality Check

  • Grapes: Pure sugar bombs, minimal nutrients
  • Bananas: 14g sugar each, spikes insulin
  • Mangoes: 46g sugar per fruit
  • Pineapple: Extremely high glycemic
  • Fruit juice: Liquid sugar, no fiber
  • Dried fruit: Concentrated sugar + sulfites
If You Eat Fruit:
  • Only in season (fall harvest)
  • Small portions
  • Choose berries (lowest sugar)
  • Always with protein/fat
  • Never juice
  • Limit to 1-2 servings daily max
